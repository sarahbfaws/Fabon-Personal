RSPCA

Copywriting | Photography

Overview

‣
I volunteered for the RSPCA digital marketing team during COVID in 2020 as a creative writer. During this experience, I wrote copy for the official RSPCA QLD blog as well as the social media pages.
‣
As an avid animal lover, it was special to contribute my skills to a cause that I was passionate about. For this particular article, I was provided with a press release about RSPCA's partnership with Re.Group for the launch of their recycling application. You can visit the above article here →

Process

‣
Hannah reached out to me for assistance with designing and deploying a new website to reach new clients for her psychology services. I was provided with her chosen domain's website editor on Crazy Domains. Although there are limitations that come with a website builder, I was committed to creating a website that looked cohesive and professinal.
‣
Hannah requested to remain on a website builder so that she could have easy access to edit the website as needed. Despite limitations that come with a website builder, I was determined to make the website look as cohesive and professional as possible.

Photography

‣
In this project, I wanted to provide meaningful images to support the article. As such, with assistance from the family dogs, I staged some images to accompany my writing.
‣
After submitting the article and images to the marketing coordinator, I receieved positive feedback, and inspired the team to build up a collection of lifestyle images for future articles.
