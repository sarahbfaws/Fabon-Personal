HANNAH ROSE PSYCHOLOGY

Web Design | Web Development | Copywriting | Photography

Overview

‣
Hannah Rose Psychology is a nurturing psychology practice with a cosy studio in Paddington. Hannah's core mission is to provide psychological support that is rooted in kindness and compassion, empowering people to flourish.
‣
It was an honour to assist Hannah by volunteering my skills with website design, development, photography, and copywriting. Since Hannah was in the process of branching out from an existing practice to start her own business, it was essential to accurately capture her warmth and kindness to connect her with clients in need.

Process

‣
Hannah reached out to me for assistance with designing and deploying a new website to reach new clients for her psychology services. I was provided with her chosen domain's website editor on Crazy Domains. Although there are limitations that come with a website builder, such as the responsive design, I was committed to creating a website that looked cohesive and professinal.
‣
The first step in the process was to meet with Hannah and discuss her vision for the website. I prepared a short questionnaire and a list of actionable tasks. From here, I helped Hannah define her goals and arrive at the core of what makes her business unique. We identified that we wanted to highlight her cosy Paddington studio as a warm and welcoming environment, as well as her trained therapy dog Clove, as something that sets her apart from the average psychology practice. This helped direct the design process as I had a central theme and aesthetic to refer to.

Overall Contribution

✓
Meeting over coffee to discuss vision, goals, draft the FAQ's, and answer any questions.
✓
Photography session.
✓
Complete gallery of 38 edited images for use anywhere. This included portraits, images of the studio, and images of the therapy dog Clove.
✓
Completed website, including design and copywriting.
✓
Home page, About Page, FAQ page, Contact Page, Services & Fees page.

Photography

‣
When meeting with Hannah, we had highlighted her cosy studio as a selling point for her psychology practice. With this in mind, I offered to bring my camera to her studio and take some photos so that we could highlight this environment and also show off her beautiful therapy dog Clove.
‣
Having intentional images that match the website's aesthetic and design immediately elevated the website. The colours of her studio are cohesive with her brand colours, and we were able to accurately depict the feeling of walking into the studio space.
‣
Not only are the photos visually appealing, but it was also important for Hannah that her future clients understood exactly what to expect when forming their decision to visit her practice.
